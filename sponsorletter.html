As the lead sponsoring nonprofit, Discovery Cube OC, part of the Discovery Science Foundation, cordially invites you to support the 2016 South County Water Expo!

This special event is being presented by OC Board of Supervisors Chairwoman Lisa Bartlett in partnership with OC Environmental Resources, the City of Mission Viejo, the City of San Clemente, City of San Juan Capistrano, El Toro Water District, Irvine Ranch Water District, Laguna Beach County Water District, Moulton Niguel Water District, Municipal Water District of Orange County, Santa Margarita Water District, South Coast Water District, and Trabuco Canyon Water District. The South County Water Expo will be held Saturday, October 1 at the Norman P. Murray Center, 24932 Veterans Way, Mission Viejo, CA 92692.

By becoming a sponsor of the inaugural South County Water Expo, you will be recognized in the Event Program that attendees will receive upon arrival. In addition, your tax-deductible donation will provide your Organization the options and incentives listed in the enclosed Sponsor Form. Your involvement will also demonstrate your appreciation for the importance of the provision of clean water, as well as water conservation and sustainability.

Discovery Cube is the leading provider of water conservation education in Southern California. Through partnerships with over 50% of Metropolitan Water’s Districts assessed valuation, the Cube provides water conservation education to over 200,000 students annually.

Thousands of community members are expected to attend the South County Water Expo, which will take place from 10 AM to 2 PM, beginning with a welcome address by Supervisor Lisa Bartlett and followed by informative presentations by renowned water experts. The Water Expo will also feature educational and interactive booths, as well as fun activities and food for the whole family.

Thank you for your consideration of this sponsorship request. To become a sponsor of the 2016 South County Water Expo, please complete a Sponsor Pledge Form.
